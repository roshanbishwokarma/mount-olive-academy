ABOUT MOUNT OLIVE

A History of Academic Distinction & Culture

Established in 2057 BS (2000 AD) at Godawari Municipality-3, Kailali, Nepal, Mount Olive Academy has spent over two decades as a pillar of educational excellence.

Our philosophy revolves around holistic development. We combine strict academic standards with rich extracurricular cultural activities to make learning an inspiring journey. We focus on building modern skills, character, and creative problem-solving capability.

"A center of education, culture and inspiration is Our Motto."

"To Product Civilized and quality people."

Our World-Class Facilities

We provide an environment optimized for learning, safety, and physical development.

Modern Library

A peaceful study space stocked with extensive textbooks, journals, reference materials, and e-learning resources.

Science Labs

Fully-equipped Chemistry, Physics, and Biology labs enabling students to perform hands-on scientific experiments safely.

Computer Center

A high-tech lab featuring modern operating systems, high-speed internet, and customized programming tools.

Sports Ground

Expansive playgrounds catering to football, cricket, basketball, volleyball, and physical fitness activities.

Hygienic Canteen

Serving fresh, nutritious, and balanced meals prepared with strict hygiene standards for students and faculty.

ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE

Academic Wings & Curricula

Our curriculum is tailored to promote academic rigour and creative competence across all grade levels.

Grades Nursery - 5

Primary Wing

Focuses on foundational numeracy, literacy, socialization, and cognitive coordination through interactive, play-based activities.

  • Play-based learning methods
  • Creative art and music classes
  • Individual student attention
Grades 6 - 8

Middle School

Broadens conceptual understanding in core sciences, mathematics, humanities, and introduces foundational computer programming.

  • Lab-oriented science learning
  • Analytical mathematics syllabus
  • Introduction to Python & coding
Grades 9 - 10

Secondary Wing

Focused preparation for secondary school assessments and the Secondary Education Examination (SEE), ensuring a strong academic foundation.

  • SEE preparation workshops
  • Specialized secondary curriculum
  • Academic counseling seminars
